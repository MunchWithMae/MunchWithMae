This is one of my absolute favorite meals to cook. It's an earthy, umami-packed curry that can be paired with almost any protein or vegetable. I've made it with pork, chicken thighs, amd beef & it has come out spectacular every time. When i've made it without protein, my usual go-to vegetables are broccoli, corn, potato, and onion. If you accidentally get it too spicy, you can save it by adding a little more brown sugar to balance it out. Don't skip out on the coriander ; It really brightens up the flavor and gives it the necessary flavor profile to combat the spiciness.

Ingredients

1 lb Ground beef


Directions

Cook your rice in a rice cooker, adding your seasonings: salt, pepper, garlic powder, and cayenne to the water.
In a medium - high heat pan, add ground beef, let sear off for a minute or two, then add your sliced onions
