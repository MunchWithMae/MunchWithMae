{Mission}

Munch With Mae is a simple cooking website I started aimed at accessability and affordability. My goal is to teach you the basics of cooking through some simple recipes to help get you started on your cooking career without all the pretentiousness you find on many cooking websites today.

I aim to provide you with some excellent recipes that people of all skill levels can master with a little time and effort and without all the costly grandoise ingredients you see on so many websites today. You won't find any truffle oil or 24 carat gold flakes to put on top of your caviar here!

My recipes are flavour focused and while simple, will leave you feeling like a master chef in no time. You will have no trouble following our detailed step-by-step recipes that gently guide you through the entire meal prep and cooking process, adding in small tips to further your knowledge along the way.

I hope you have fun on your cooking adventures and I wish you nothing but the best!

{About Me}

My name is Mae and i'm a self taught amateur chef. I started cooking around mid 2017. I was inspired to start learning when I got a job as a waitress at a steakhouse. It was one of those steakhouses where you could see into the grill area of the kitchen from the lobby and you could always see big flames coming from the grill.

I eventually started training on the grill on some of the slower days grilling items such as steaks, salmon, veggie skewers, etc. I found that I really enjoyed it, so I got a grill for my home and began my cooking journey.

From grilling, my next branch was simply adding things to ramen and trying to find ways to make it more appealing (being broke will do that to you). This lead me to discovering flavor profiles and figuring out what worked well together. It's all history from there.

If you enjoy my content, consider giving me a follow on instagram. I'd also love to see photos of anything you've cooked!
